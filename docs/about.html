About Home Handyman Solutions LLC

Our Story

Founded with a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, Home Handyman Solutions LLC has grown from a local contractor to a trusted name across Nebraska and Iowa. Our journey began with a simple philosophy: deliver quality workmanship, honest pricing, and reliable service to every client.

Meet Michael Lenz - Founder & Chief Engineer

Michael brings over 8 years of professional maintenance and construction experience to every project. As a Missouri Valley, Iowa native, he understands the unique challenges that properties in our region face. His extensive background spans from large-scale hotel maintenance operations to personalized handyman services.

Professional Excellence

Michael's career highlights include serving as Chief Engineer at major hospitality properties, where he successfully rebuilt maintenance departments, improved efficiency, and consistently passed brand standard audits. His approach focuses on quality over speed - delivering lasting results that reduce future maintenance needs.

Since launching his handyman business in 2020, Michael has been building a loyal clientele throughout the Omaha-Council Bluffs area. His diverse skill set allows him to handle everything from complex HVAC systems to detailed electrical work, always maintaining the highest standards of safety and craftsmanship.
